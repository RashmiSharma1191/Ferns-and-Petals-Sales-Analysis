## ğŸ“Š Key Insights & Business Recommendations
- Key Insights
  - Peak Demand Cycles: Revenue peaks in August and on Tuesdays, indicating periods of high consumer engagement.
  - Top Performers: The 'Colors' category is the highest revenue generator, and the 'Magnam Set' is the leading individual product.
  - Customer Timing: Orders peak around 9:00 AM. Males spend the most on 'Anniversary' occasions.
  - Delivery Metric: The average order delivery time stands at 5.53 days.
- Business Recommendations
  - Targeted Marketing: Increase marketing spend and run targeted promotions before August and specifically on Tuesdays to capitalize on peak demand.
  - Inventory Focus: Prioritize stocking and merchandising of the 'Colors' category and the 'Magnam Set' to ensure consistent revenue flow.
  - Engagement Strategy: Schedule promotions around 9:00 AM. Create premium product bundles specifically targeting male customers for 'Anniversary' purchases.
  - Service Enhancement: Implement measures to reduce the average delivery time to below 4 days to significantly boost customer satisfaction.
